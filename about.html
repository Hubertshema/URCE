About Us

Welcome to the Student Marks Checker, a web-based portal built to simplify the retrieval of academic results for the University of Rwanda, College of Education (UR-CE) community at the Rukara Campus.

Our Purpose

This system was developed to replace traditional paper and manual spreadsheet-based result checking with a fast, secure, and easily accessible digital experience. It enables student-teachers and academic personnel to view performance records instantly while adhering to privacy and academic policies.

Development & Administration

This digital prototype is maintained to aid in the digitization of academic resources. To improve transparency and tracking, the system uses internal logging procedures that record the registration numbers of users making queries, keeping unauthorized users away from personal names.
